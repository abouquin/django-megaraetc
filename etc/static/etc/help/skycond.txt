** SKY CONDITION **
Options	are	Photometric, Clear, and	Spectroscopic.
In magnitudes,
Photometric	= +0,
Clear = +0.4, Spectroscopic = +1.
This value is added to the overall system efficiency,
and	corresponds to 100% efficiency for photometric,
69.2% for clear, and 39.8% for spectroscopic sky condition.
